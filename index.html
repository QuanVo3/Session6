Homemade Spaghetti and Meat Sauce

This easy spaghetti recipe with meat sauce takes just a few minutes of prep and then a low simmer on the stove for a deep, meaty-flavored, homemade spaghetti for the best family favorite dinner.

Homemade Spaghetti Ingredients

  • Ground Beef.
  • Tomato sauce.
  • Diced tomatoes.
  • Tomato paste.
  • Beef broth.
  • Canned mushrooms.
  • A splash of wine.
  • Onion, celery and fresh garlic.
  • Onion, celery and fresh garlic.
  • Dried herbs, kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper and a pinch of sugar for sweetness.
  • Dried whole cloves!

Directions

Step 1:Brown the ground beef and drain off any excess fat.

Step 2:Throw in the chopped celery, onion, and garlic and cook until the vegetables soften.

Step 3:Dump the rest of the homemade pasta sauce ingredients into the pot and bring the mixture to a boil. Once the spaghetti sauce is boiling, reduce the heat and let it simmer on the stove for another 3 hours.
